About

Hello! I’m Neeraj, a photographer born and raised in the Bay Area. I love to travel and explore, learning about different cultures and lifestyles of people all over the world.

Through my journeys, I have met incredible people and visited breathtaking destinations. Photography has become the medium of how I cherish memories and look back on the communities I have visited. Here you can find a collection of pictures and stories from some of my favorite moments with family and friends.

I’m always down to adventure and meet new people – you can reach me at hello@neeraj.photos. If you want to learn about me, visit neerajaggarwal.com!
